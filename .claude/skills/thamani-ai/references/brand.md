
# Thamani Brand Identity

You are the voice and visual guardian for Thamani. Every word, image, and design choice
represents a brand built on financial mindfulness for East Africa's M-PESA users. This
document is the single source of truth for how Thamani looks, sounds, and feels.

## Brand Foundation

| Element | Definition |
|---------|-----------|
| **Mission** | Make financial awareness clear, rewarding, and completely private for every M-PESA user |
| **Vision** | A world where looking at your money brings clarity, not anxiety |
| **Values** | Knowledge, Simplicity, Community, Purpose |
| **Positioning** | The financial mindfulness app for M-PESA users — not an accounting tool, but an awareness companion |
| **Personality** | Insightful, encouraging, clear, trustworthy, warm |

## Brand Tagline

**Build Future. Build Value. Build Clarity. Build Freedom. BUILD THAMANI.**

Use the full chain for hero moments (splash, onboarding, about page). Use individual
fragments for contextual moments:
- Onboarding: "Build Clarity"
- Streak achievement: "Build Freedom"
- Savings goal reached: "Build Value"
- General CTA: "Build Thamani"

## Voice Attributes

Thamani's voice is consistent across all channels. These six attributes define how we sound:

| Attribute | What It Means | What It's Not |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **Insightful** | We reveal patterns and meaning in numbers — "you spent more" becomes "here's why" | Data-dumping or showing off analytics jargon |
| **Encouraging** | Every interaction nudges toward better habits, never shame | Patronizing, preachy, or guilt-tripping about spending |
| **Clear** | Short sentences. Plain language. No financial jargon unless explained | Oversimplified or dumbed-down |
| **Trustworthy** | Precise numbers, honest assessments, never exaggerate | Cold, robotic, or overly formal |
| **Warm** | We speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a bank or institution | Overly casual, slangy, or unprofessional |
| **Private** | We treat financial data as sacred; language reinforces user control | Surveillance-like or invasive |

## Tone Matrix

The voice stays the same; the tone shifts by context:

| Context | Tone Dial | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Insights & reports** | Calm, revealing, matter-of-fact | "You spent 15% more on Food this week. Here's the breakdown." |
| **Streak achievements** | Celebratory, warm, motivating | "7 days of clarity! You've unlocked your Weekly Reflection." |
| **Budget warnings** | Honest, supportive, non-judgmental | "You're at 85% of your Dining limit. 3 days left this week." |
| **Broken streaks** | Gentle, forward-looking, never shaming | "Your streak reset. Every new day is a fresh start." |
| **Error messages** | Calm, helpful, solution-oriented | "We couldn't sync your transactions. Tap to retry." |
| **Onboarding** | Excited, simple, welcoming | "Let's turn your M-PESA messages into clarity." |
| **Push notifications** | Concise, actionable, curiosity-driven | "Your weekly reflection is ready. Tap to see your patterns." |
| **Marketing / social** | Confident, aspirational, relatable | "Your money tells a story. Thamani helps you read it." |
| **Legal / privacy** | Precise, transparent, reassuring | "Your data never leaves your device. Period." |

## Words & Phrases

### Use These

| Word/Phrase | Why |
|---|---|
| "clarity" | Core brand concept — we bring clarity to finances |
| "insight" | We don't just show data, we reveal meaning |
| "your money story" | Personalizes the financial journey |
| "patterns" | Implies intelligence without jargon |
| "Build [X]" | Brand tagline fragments — always aspirational |
| "streak" / "shield" | Gamification vocabulary — owned by Thamani |
| "mindful" / "mindfulness" | Core brand positioning |
| "KES" | Always use KES, not Ksh or K or Kshs |

### Avoid These

| Word/Phrase | Why | Use Instead |
|---|---|---|
| "budget" (as verb) | Feels restrictive and boring | "set a limit" or "protect your spending" |
| "track" | Implies surveillance | "understand" or "see" |
| "spend less" | Shame-adjacent | "spend smarter" or "find balance" |
| "alert" | Feels alarming | "heads up" or "update" |
| "user" | Impersonal | "you" (direct address) |
| "data" | Cold, technical | "your money" or "your transactions" |
| "syncing" | Technical | "reading your messages" or "updating" |
| "error" | Scary | "something went wrong" or specific issue |
| "Ksh" / "K" / "Kshs" | Inconsistent | Always "KES" |

## Number & Currency Formatting

| Format | Example | When |
|---|---|---|
| Currency | KES 2,500 | Always space after KES, comma thousands separator |
| Percentage | 15% | No space before %, always whole numbers unless precision matters |
| Dates | Mar 12, 2026 | Abbreviated month, no leading zeros |
| Times | 3:42 PM | 12-hour, uppercase AM/PM |
| Streaks | 7-day streak | Hyphenated, lowercase "day" |

## Typography

### Font: Geist

Thamani uses **Geist** by Vercel as its primary typeface across all platforms. Geist is
modern, highly legible, and optimized for digital interfaces — a perfect match for a
mobile-first fintech product.

| Platform | Primary | Mono | Fallback |
|---|---|---|---|
| Android | `Geist` | `Geist Mono` | `Roboto`, system sans-serif |
| iOS | `Geist` | `Geist Mono` | `SF Pro`, system sans-serif |
| Web | `Geist` | `Geist Mono` | `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif` |

**Why Geist:**
- Clean geometric shapes communicate precision and trust
- Excellent legibility at small sizes on mobile screens
- Geist Mono provides perfect tabular numerals for financial amounts
- Open source, no licensing constraints across platforms

**Money amounts** always use `Geist Mono` with `tabular-nums` for aligned digits.

## Logo System

### The Mark

The Thamani logo is three overlapping paths forming a unified shape, representing the
convergence of insight, discipline, and growth. The three colors (Deep Blue, Mid Blue, Cyan)
flow from trust to possibility.

### Approved Variations

| Variation | Usage |
|---|---|
| **Full color on white** | Primary — use for most contexts |
| **Full color on dark** | Dark backgrounds, dark mode |
| **Monochrome dark** | Single-color printing, minimal contexts |
| **Monochrome light** | On dark surfaces, photography overlays |
| **Mark only** | App icon, favicons, small spaces |

### Logo Rules

**Clear space:** Maintain minimum clear space equal to the width of the smallest logo
path on all sides.

**Minimum size:**
- Digital: 32px height (mark only), 120px width (full logo)
- Print: 10mm height (mark only), 30mm width (full logo)

**Prohibited uses:**
- Do not stretch, rotate, or distort
- Do not apply drop shadows, outlines, or additional effects
- Do not recolor individual paths — use approved variations only
- Do not place on busy photographic backgrounds without a color block
- Do not use the gradient on non-brand surfaces

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style

| Dimension | Guideline |
|---|---|
| **People** | Authentic East African faces and settings — real life, not staged stock |
| **Lighting** | Warm, natural light — Kenya's golden hour feel |
| **Settings** | Markets, matatu stops, dukas, homes, campuses — where M-PESA lives |
| **Devices** | Show real phones naturally — not perfectly positioned product shots |
| **Color treatment** | Warm tones preferred; can overlay brand gradient at low opacity |

### Illustration Style
- Clean, geometric, flat design with brand colors only
- Rounded corners and soft shapes — approachable, not corporate
- Limited palette: primary trio + neutrals + one semantic color per illustration
- No photorealistic 3D renders or cartoonish characters

### Iconography
- Stroke-based, 1.5px weight at 24px size
- Rounded caps and joins
- Use `neutral-700` default, `primary` for active/selected
- Consistent optical sizing across the set

### Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Show real Kenyan/East African contexts | Use generic global stock photography |
| Feature M-PESA green alongside brand blue | Use competitor brand colors |
| Show phones in natural hand positions | Show perfectly staged device mockups |
| Use warm, natural lighting | Use cold, corporate studio lighting |
| Feature diverse ages and settings | Default to only urban young professionals |

## Notification & In-App Copy Templates

### Insight Notifications
```
Pattern: "[Observation]. [Implication or action]."
Example: "You spent 15% more on Food this week. Tap to see the breakdown."
Example: "Transport costs dropped by KES 800. Your commute change is paying off."
```

### Streak Notifications
```
Pattern: "[Achievement]! [What it unlocks or means]."
Example: "7-day streak! Your Weekly Reflection is ready."
Example: "30 days of clarity! You've earned Master of Clarity."
```

### Budget Warnings
```
Pattern: "[Status]. [Context without judgment]."
Example: "You're at 85% of your Dining limit. 3 days left this week."
Example: "Entertainment shield is holding strong. KES 200 remaining."
```

### Error States
```
Pattern: "[What happened]. [What to do]."
Example: "We couldn't read your latest messages. Tap to retry."
Example: "Something went wrong loading your insights. Pull down to refresh."
```

## Brand Audit Checklist

When reviewing any Thamani asset, verify:

- [ ] Colors match the approved palette (no off-brand blues or grays)
- [ ] Font is Geist (not Inter, Roboto, or system default)
- [ ] Currency formatted as "KES X,XXX" (not Ksh, K, or Kshs)
- [ ] Copy follows voice attributes (insightful, encouraging, clear)
- [ ] No shame-language about spending ("spend less", "wasting money")
- [ ] Insight-first framing (answers, not raw data)
- [ ] Logo has proper clear space and is an approved variation
- [ ] Imagery feels authentically East African
- [ ] Privacy language reinforces user control
- [ ] Error states are calm and solution-oriented
